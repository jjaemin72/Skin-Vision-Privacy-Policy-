Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2/24/25

1. Introduction

Welcome to Skin Vision! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

2. Face Data Collection and Usage

Skin Vision processes face data strictly for user convenience. The app collects and stores face data locally on the user’s device. No face data is transmitted, shared, or processed externally.

What Face Data Does the App Collect?

The app processes facial images captured by the user for analysis and display within the app.

How Is the Collected Face Data Used?

Face data is used solely for on-device visualization and analysis. Users can access their own face data but it is not used for any other purpose.

Is Face Data Shared with Third Parties?

No. Face data is never shared with third parties and is stored exclusively on the user's device.

How Long Is Face Data Retained?

Face data remains on the user’s device as long as they choose to keep it. The user has full control over deletion.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Skin Vision may collect anonymized analytics data to improve app performance, but this does not include any identifiable user information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics and app improvement. These services do not access or store any facial data.

5. Security

We prioritize your security by ensuring that no sensitive personal data is collected or stored. Your privacy is our top concern.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
